PRACTICE AREAS

Civil Law

Representation of individuals and legal entities regarding discussions related to rights and obligations, corporate law, possession and property, contracts, consumer law, and civil liability before State and Federal Courts.

Family and Succession Law

Representation in cases related to family and succession law, including marriage, divorce, recognition and dissolution of common-law marriage, adoption, custody, alimony, guardianship, curatorship, judicial and extrajudicial probate.

Real Estate Law

Providing services for the drafting of legal instruments related to real estate transactions, including purchase and sale agreements, exchange agreements, donations, deed in lieu, construction contracts, lease agreements, and tenancy agreements.

Analysis and regularization of real estate documentation with notary publics and land registries.

Litigation in possessory and reivindicatory actions, such as eviction, repossession and delivery of possession, ordinary, extraordinary, and special adverse possession, compulsory adjudication, and contract terminations.

Labor Law

Preventive and litigation legal advice in defense of employers' interests before trade unions, the Ministry of Labor, and the Labor Court.

Defense of employees' interests in individual actions before the Labor Court.

Visas and Foreigners' Assistance

Legalization of foreigners in Brazil. Assistance in obtaining visas for foreign clients. Employment analysis for foreign clients.

Legal Consulting

Issuance of legal opinions on matters related to the aforementioned practice areas.
